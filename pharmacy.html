Care Management Programs

Programs tailored for pharmacy-based care delivery

CCM

Chronic Care Management

Manage patients with multiple chronic conditions through regular medication reviews and care coordination.

  • Medication therapy management
  • Monthly care coordination calls
  • Adherence monitoring and support
  • Care plan updates
RPM

Remote Patient Monitoring

Monitor patients with hypertension, diabetes, and other conditions using connected devices.

  • Blood pressure monitors
  • Glucose meters
  • Weight scales
  • Pulse oximeters
MTM

Medication Therapy Management

Comprehensive medication reviews that complement care management programs.

  • Drug interaction review
  • Therapy optimization
  • Patient education
  • Provider communication
