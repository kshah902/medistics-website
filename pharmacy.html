Pharmacy Care Capabilities

How pharmacies participate in care management programs

Medication Monitoring

Medication Adherence Support

Pharmacies play a critical role in medication adherence‚Äîone of the most important factors in chronic disease management.

Key Activities

  • Medication synchronization and refill coordination
  • Adherence monitoring and intervention
  • Drug interaction and safety reviews
  • Patient education on proper medication use
  • Communication with prescribers on therapy issues
Care Coordination

Provider Collaboration

Pharmacies serve as an accessible touchpoint for patients, enabling more frequent engagement and monitoring between provider visits.

Key Activities

  • Patient check-ins and wellness monitoring
  • Blood pressure and glucose screenings
  • Immunization administration and tracking
  • Care gap identification and closure
  • Coordination with provider care teams
RPM Support

Device Distribution & Support

Pharmacies can serve as convenient distribution points for RPM devices and provide ongoing patient support.

  • Device pickup and patient training
  • Troubleshooting and device replacement
  • Reading review and patient follow-up
Patient Education

Chronic Disease Education

Leverage pharmacy expertise to educate patients on managing chronic conditions and medication therapy.

  • Disease-specific patient education
  • Lifestyle modification counseling
  • Medication therapy optimization
