Clinical Services That Drive Pharmacy Revenue

Community pharmacies are uniquely positioned to improve patient outcomes. You see patients more frequently than their doctors. You understand their medications better than anyone. And patients trust you.

But translating that position into sustainable revenue has been challenging. Traditional dispensing margins continue to shrink. MTM programs have limited reach. And building provider partnerships is time-consuming.

Medistics Pharmacy Enablement changes this equation. We connect pharmacies with medical providers who need help managing their care management programs‚Äîcreating structured partnerships where pharmacies deliver clinical services and share in the resulting Medicare revenue.

The opportunity: Medical practices have thousands of patients eligible for care management programs but lack the staff to reach them. Pharmacies have clinical expertise and patient relationships. Medistics brings them together.

Services Pharmacies Can Deliver

Through provider partnerships, pharmacies can participate in multiple care management activities:

Comprehensive Medication Review

Full review of all medications for appropriateness, interactions, duplications, and optimization opportunities.

Medication Reconciliation

Ensure accurate medication lists after hospitalizations, specialist visits, or other transitions of care.

Adherence Monitoring

Track refill patterns, conduct adherence calls, and address barriers to consistent medication taking.

Patient Education

Condition-specific education, medication counseling, and self-management support.

Device Training (RPM)

Help patients set up and use remote monitoring devices like blood pressure monitors and glucose meters.

Care Coordination

Communicate findings to prescribers, facilitate medication changes, and coordinate with care teams.
