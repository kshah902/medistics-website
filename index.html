How Medistics Works

A streamlined process from identification to billing

1

Identify Eligible Patients

We continuously analyze EHR data to identify patients eligible for care management programs based on diagnoses, utilization, and payer rules.

2

Enroll Patients

Patients are enrolled through compliant phone, SMS, or in-practice workflows with documented consent.

3

Engage & Monitor

Patients receive ongoing outreach, monitoring, and education through AI-assisted workflows and connected devices when applicable.
